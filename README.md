## 📖 Project Overview

Blinkit is one of India's leading quick-commerce platforms, serving customers through a network of outlets and a wide range of product categories.

This Power BI project focuses on analyzing sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution across Blinkit's outlets to uncover actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor critical KPIs, evaluate outlet performance, understand customer preferences, and identify opportunities for business optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's:

* Sales Performance
* Customer Satisfaction
* Inventory Distribution
* Outlet Performance

using interactive Power BI dashboards and KPI-driven reporting.

---

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI                | Description                                      |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| 💰 Total Sales     | Overall revenue generated from all products sold |
| 📈 Average Sales   | Average revenue generated per transaction        |
| 📦 Number of Items | Total number of products sold                    |
| ⭐ Average Rating   | Average customer rating across products          |

---

# 🔍 Business Analysis Performed

## 1️⃣ Total Sales by Fat Content

### Objective

Analyze how product fat content impacts overall sales performance.

### Metrics Analyzed

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items Sold
* Average Rating

### Business Value

Helps understand customer purchasing behavior towards Low Fat and Regular products.

---

## 2️⃣ Total Sales by Item Type

### Objective

Identify the highest and lowest-performing product categories.

### Metrics Analyzed

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items Sold
* Average Rating

### Business Value

Supports inventory planning and category-level business strategy.

---

## 3️⃣ Fat Content Analysis by Outlet

### Objective

Compare outlet performance based on the sales contribution of Low Fat and Regular products.

### Metrics Analyzed

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items Sold
* Average Rating

### Business Value

Provides insights into product demand patterns across outlets.

---

## 4️⃣ Total Sales by Outlet Establishment Year

### Objective

Evaluate how outlet age and establishment period influence sales performance.

### Business Value

Helps determine whether older or newer outlets contribute more revenue.

---

# 📈 Visual Analytics

## 5️⃣ Percentage of Sales by Outlet Size

### Objective

Analyze the relationship between outlet size and sales generation.

### Business Questions Answered

* Which outlet size contributes the highest revenue?
* Do larger outlets generate significantly more sales?

### Visualization

* Donut Chart

---

## 6️⃣ Sales by Outlet Location

### Objective

Assess geographical sales distribution across outlet locations.

### Business Questions Answered

* Which locations generate the highest sales?
* How does customer demand vary by region?

### Visualization

* Funnel Chart / Bar Chart

---

## 7️⃣ Performance Metrics by Outlet Type

### Objective

Provide a comprehensive comparison of all outlet types.

### Metrics Included

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items Sold
* Average Rating

### Business Questions Answered

* Which outlet type performs best overall?
* Which outlet type has the highest customer satisfaction?
* Which outlet type contributes the largest share of revenue?

### Visualization

* Matrix Table
* Bar Charts
